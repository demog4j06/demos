The Origin of AE SEXY

At first, AE was a company specializing in video streaming technology for large clients. With the sophisticated expertises and excellent services the company offered, it has become more and more well-known in the industry, landed many major clients with their trust and support, and established a great reputation for itself despite the compact size. One day, AE received a request from one of their clients, and planned a trip all the way to Mexico to build a live interactive studio. However, every cloud has a silver lining, AE made a decision to continue working on this project to keep the ball rolling, and set out to establish their very own brand in the entertainment industry. With decades of experiences providing sophisticated technologies for their clients and featuring sexy bikini croupiers, AE has established an outstanding branding image in the industry of entertainment.
